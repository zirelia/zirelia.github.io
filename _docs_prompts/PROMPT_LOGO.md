# Prompt per la Generazione del Logo - KineticMCP

Usa questi prompt con strumenti come **Midjourney**, **DALL-E 3**, o **Ideogram**. Scegli lo stile che preferisci.

---

## Opzione 1: Minimalista & Astratto (Consigliato)
*Ideale per un look moderno, pulito e scalabile.*

> **Prompt:**
> A modern, minimalist tech logo for a project named "KineticMCP". The design should feature an abstract geometric shape representing kinetic energy, motion, and connection. Use a color palette of electric blue and deep slate gray. The icon should be sleek, vector-style, flat design, suitable for a SaaS product or developer tool. White background.

## Opzione 2: Futuristico & "Neon Glow"
*Ideale per sottolineare l'aspetto AI e High-Tech.*

> **Prompt:**
> A futuristic, glowing logo symbol for "KineticMCP". The concept is a high-tech bridge or data node connecting two worlds (AI and Database). Cyberpunk aesthetic but professional. Neon cyan and purple gradients on a dark background. The shape should imply speed and data transfer. High definition, 3D render style but clean enough for an icon.

## Opzione 3: Corporate & Trustworthy (Stile Enterprise)
*Ideale se il target sono grandi aziende e Salesforce Architects.*

> **Prompt:**
> A professional corporate logo for "KineticMCP", a Salesforce integration tool. The symbol should be a stylized letter "K" integrated with a cloud or database icon. solid colors, navy blue and white. Trustworthy, stable, engineering-focused. Vector graphics, bold lines, sans-serif typography for the text "KineticMCP".

## Opzione 4: Concettuale (Il Ponte)
*Focalizzato sulla funzione di "connessione" del protocollo MCP.*

> **Prompt:**
> An isometric icon design for "KineticMCP". The visual metaphor is a sleek, modern connector plug or a digital bridge linking a robot arm (AI) to a cloud server. Clean lines, tech-blue and silver colors. Isometric view, 3D vector style, white background.

---

### Consigli per la rifinitura
*   Se usi **Midjourney**, aggiungi `--no text` se vuoi solo l'icona e poi aggiungere il testo tu.
*   Se usi **DALL-E 3**, chiedigli esplicitamente di separare l'icona dal testo.
